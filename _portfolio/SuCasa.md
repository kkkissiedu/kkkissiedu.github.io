## Project Overview

This project involved the complete structural analysis of a Pratt truss bridge designed for pedestrian and light vehicle traffic. The primary objective was to ensure safety, stability, and serviceability under various load combinations.

## Software Used
* **Modeling & Analysis:** SAP2000
* **Drafting:** AutoCAD

## Key Responsibilities
* Created the 3D structural model in SAP2000.
* Applied dead loads, live loads, and wind loads as per Eurocode 3.
* Performed linear static analysis to determine member forces and deflections.
* Verified the structural integrity of all steel members.
